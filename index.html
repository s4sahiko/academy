Future Skills

Centre for Future Skills

Centre for Future Skills is an initiative of the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship through NSDC to ensure availability of technology infrastructure and experts on latest technologies in the college campus. EthnoTech Academy is an exclusive partner of NSDC to implement this project.

The CFS is a futuristic state of the art technology infrastructure for the students to stay up to date with all the latest advanced technologies that the industry demands.
